Bring her back(2025)

Directed by Danny Philippou & Michael Philippou

A brother and sister discover that their foster mother is hiding a dark secret in their remote family home. After the sister mysteriously disappears, the brother is left alone with a terrifying ritual that his foster mother has been performing for years one that brings the dead back to life. But what comes back is never quite the same. As he races to save his sister before she becomes the next victim of the ritual, he uncovers a cycle of grief, obsession, and horror that has been repeating for generations. Bring Her Back is a relentless nightmare about the lengths people will go to hold onto the ones they love.


Cast

  • Sally Hawkins as The Mother
  • Billy Barratt as The Brother
  • Siena Agudong as The Sister
  • Jonah Wren Phillips as Young Boy

Why do I like this movie

Bring Her Back is the kind of horror that gets under your skin and stays there. From the directors of Talk to Me, this film takes the familiar concept of grief-fueled horror and twists it into something deeply unsettling. The atmosphere is suffocating, the tension never lets up, and Sally Hawkins delivers a chilling performance that will haunt you long after the credits roll. It's not just about jump scares—it's about the horror of loss, the desperation of denial, and the terrifying question: what if bringing someone back means losing yourself? It's smart, brutal, and unforgettable.
